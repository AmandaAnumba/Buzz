About Buzz

Overview

Buzz makes it easier to view what pop-culture topics are trending on Twitter. It provides city-specific data about trending pop culture topics by dynamically harvesting keywords and displaying media links related to each keyword. Simply pick a city and discover what pop culture icons are buzzing!

Music Trends

Want an easier way to make sense of what songs and artists are trending on Twitter? Check out Music Trends and discover local bands or which music genres are top trending in each city.

Discover

what's buzzing in a specific city. Select a city to view the top trending pop culture icons and the related media (YouTube videos, news links, and images)

Explore

what's buzzing in all cities by navigating to the explore page to view the representation of tweets in a city out of all tweets in the database.
