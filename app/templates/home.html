About Buzz

Overview

Buzz makes it easier to view what's trending on Twitter. It provides city-specific data about trending pop culture topics by dynamically harvesting keywords and displaying media links related to each keyword. Simply pick a city and discover what pop culture icons are buzzing!

Music Jams

Discover

what's buzzing in a specific city by selecting a city to view the top trending pop culture icons and the related media (YouTube videos, news links, and images)

Explore

what's buzzing in all cities by navigating to the explore page to view the representation of tweets in a city out of all tweets in the database.
