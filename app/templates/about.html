About Buzz

Buzz makes it easier to view what's trending on Twitter.It provides city-specific data about trending pop culture topics within American cities by dynamically harvesting keywords and displaying media links related to each keyword. Simply pick a city and discover what pop culture icons are buzzing!

About Buzz

On Twitter, trending topics are represented in broad categories, and usually on a national or global scale. This presents a problem of locating and presenting actual, localized twitter data and media in a relevant way and comparing what people are tweeting about based on their location?

Buzz solves this problem by providing city-specific data about trending pop culture topics within American cities by dynamically harvesting keywords. By drilling down into specific pop-culture references, Buzz allows for direct references of relevant media (YouTube© videos, news articles, specific tweets).

Additionally, when compared to the old Neighborhood Buzz , Buzz no longer relies on a topical categorizer because the best categorizer is in our brains.
