About Buzz

Buzz makes it easier to view what's trending on Twitter. It provides

city-specific data about trending pop culture topics by dynamically harvesting

keywords and displaying media links related to each keyword. Simply pick a

city and discover what pop culture icons are buzzing!

Discover

what's buzzing in a specific city by selecting a city to view the top trending

pop culture icons and the related media (YouTube videos, news links, and images)

Explore

what's buzzing in all cities by navigating to the explore page to view the representation

of tweets in a city out of all tweets in the database.

Under the Hood

On Twitter, trending topics are represented in broad categories, and usually on a national or global scale. This presents several problems: the inability to locate and present actual, localized twitter data and media in a relevant way and the incapacity to compare what people are tweeting about based on their location.

Buzz solves these problems by providing specific data about trending pop culture topics within American cities by dynamically harvesting keywords. By drilling down into specific pop-culture references, Buzz allows for direct references of relevant media (YouTube videos, news articles, specific tweets).

Additionally, when compared to the old Neighborhood Buzz , Buzz no longer relies on a topical categorizer because the best categorizer is in our brains.
